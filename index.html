How Neurovex Works

From brainwaves to action in four milliseconds.

1

Signal Capture

Non-invasive EEG sensors detect micro-voltage fluctuations on the scalp.

2

Filters & Denoise

Hardware & software filters remove muscle artifacts (EMG) and electrical noise.

3

Pattern AI

Neural networks classify intention (Focus, Relax, Motor Imagery) in real-time.

4

Assistive Output

Commands are sent to wheelchairs, home automation, or digital interfaces.

Safety & Ethics First

Neurovex strictly adheres to the Asilomar Principles for AI and medical safety standards. Our hardware includes galvanic isolation, emergency physical disconnects, and privacy-first local processing. No brain data leaves your device without explicit consent.

Neurovex is a Final Year Project for research and educational purposes. Not FDA cleared for critical life-support systems.
